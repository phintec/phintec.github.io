Jeffrey Lurie, AIA, LEED AP, Registered Architect

 

I have been in the architecture profession for about 15 years.  I received my Bachelor of Architecture Degree from the University of Miami in Coral Gables in 1994.  I became a registered architect in the State of Florida in 2001, and became a LEED Accredited Professional in 2009.  For nine years I served on the Board of Directors for the Orlando Chapter of the American Institute of Architects as Treasurer, State Director, and President for the 2009 year.


I have had the fortunate experience to work in a variety of roles - unit plan design, home design, site planning, master planning, hand drawn rendering, 3-D design in Sketchup and Revit, interior and exterior color selection and Photoshop rendering, overall building design and detailing, concept design, construction documents, construction contract administration, and most recently project team management.


I have worked on a broad range of project types from homes and apartments, hotel and restaurant renovations and new construction, timeshare resorts, student housing, office and retail.  Construction types that I have experience with in the field include wood frame, concrete tunnel form, load-bearing metal stud, steel frame, cast in place and pre-cast concrete and tilt wall.


This website will give you an overview of my work.  Most of the pictures in the primary pages are linked to secondary pages with more details and pictures.
