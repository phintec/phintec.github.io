This is an example of a visualization I did for a client that wants to add a rear porch to their two-story home.  These drawings will provide the client with material to show their Home Owners Association for initial design review purposes.  This kind of exercise can be done in a matter of hours for a predicted number of hours at an hourly rate.

When you decide to add a room onto your home, one of the most important things to figure out first is the roof intersection.  You will probably need to create new hip, ridge and valley conditions somewhere to keep all the rain draining properly.  With a visualization exercise, you can see immediately if the new arrangement will work, and how it will look.  This client had a particular image in mind using some modern forms, and this exercise showed him how his ideas could work, and where precise measurements would be needed.
